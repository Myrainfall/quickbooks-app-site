Disconnect QuickBooks

If you want to remove this application's access to QuickBooks Online, use QuickBooks or Intuit connected-app settings, or follow the disconnect steps provided by the app operator. After you disconnect, the app stops syncing new QuickBooks data right away, and any features that depend on a live QuickBooks connection will stop working until you reconnect.

Typical disconnect steps

  1. Open connected apps: Sign in to QuickBooks or Intuit and find the connected-app or app permissions settings.
  2. Find this app: Look for the QuickBooks Assistant connection in your authorized apps list.
  3. Remove access: Revoke or disconnect access. Once you do, this app can no longer pull current QuickBooks information, refresh balances or transactions, or complete actions that require your QuickBooks authorization.
