Privacy Policy

This application connects to QuickBooks Online to help the account owner review and analyze their accounting data.

In plain language: we only access the QuickBooks company information you approve, such as account, transaction, customer, vendor, or report data needed to provide the app's features.

We only access data that the user explicitly authorizes through Intuit OAuth.

We do not sell personal or financial data. We do not share QuickBooks data with third parties except as required to operate the application or comply with law.

Authorized tokens and related connection data may be stored securely for the purpose of maintaining the connection and providing requested functionality.

Users may revoke access at any time through Intuit or QuickBooks connected-app settings.
